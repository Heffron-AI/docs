---
title: 'Post-Call Messaging'
description: "Choose how you recieve conversation summaries of every call your agent picks up."
---
In the Post-call Messaging section, you can update the phone number and/or email that receives a summary message of each called picked up by your AI agent.

Choosing to receive both SMS and Email in Post-Call Messaging will result in higher credit usage.

**Post-call SMS costs**
- 10 credits for SMS
- 20 credits for Email
- Maximum of 10 post-call actions per call
