
In the Post-call Messaging section, you can update the phone number and/or email that receives a summary message of each called picked up by Heffron's AI agents.

Choosing to receive both SMS and Email in Post-Call Messaging will result in higher credit usage.

**Post-call SMS costs**
- 10 credits for SMS
- 20 credits for Email
- Maximum of 10 post-call actions per call
